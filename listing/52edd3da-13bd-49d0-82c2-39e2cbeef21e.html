The Praetoria PvE server mod pack (see: easy-install ) is focused on providing a highly challenging playthrough with Hardcore permadeath and new content new mats, gear sets, and bosses for DeepNorth Mistlands Ashlands, while also allowing players to be creative and build with the top rated building mods. the server's seasons will be 3-6months, allowing everyone plenty of time to grind out all the new content, and enjoy the end game by build some epic bases, helping new comers and crafting Perfect epic gear sets.
