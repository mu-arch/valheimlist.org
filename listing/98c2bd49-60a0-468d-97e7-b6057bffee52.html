Greetings, Viking! To join our server, you need to download the build on our website. 🌐 Our website: http://valheim-paradox.ru/ 📂 INSTALLATION Download the build, run it and specify the path that is convenient for you.

Features

📜 The PARADOX server is waiting for you: ● PARADOX PASS - free and paid pass (open pass "F10"). ● For one hour of the game you get 2 rubles, which you can spend in the donat store. To view the balance, press - "F11". ● Amulets have been changed - who is doing what in them is being tracked. ● Casino. There is a lottery in it (you can play heads and tails for coins with a Viking, the game takes place via Discord). ● Additional types of food in the tavern, which is located in the PARADOX House. ● Added tents, new flags, etc. ● List of the richest Vikings on the server. Updated every week. To get into the top 3, keep money with a banker (the list is in a separate Discord channel). ● The ability to summon bosses that are not in the vanilla version (since this is a PVP event, it has full PVP). ● MMO character leveling system. ● Professions: 8 to choose from. ● A horse has been added, you can buy it from Anastasia or in the donat store.
