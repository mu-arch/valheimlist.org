Welcome to Valheim Evolved, a place where you can experience the latest mods all in a curated environment. Join us for a fresh new adventure and start building your world now.

Features

- various new monster packs added - updated raids with new monsters - new weapons and new armor - runic gear - jewelcrafting, for powerful socketed wpns and gear - tons of QoL mods to make your life a little easier - in game player economy - quests - marketplace - better trader - mineshafts - Odin's personalized dungeons - regenerating resources, crypts, tombs, nature ...and more

About the Server

The goal of this server is to try to maintain the original vanilla feeling of strife and struggle while introducing new weapons, armor, and creatures. This is accomplished by progressively increasing the difficulty on your journey so you never feel overpowered. After having played on many different servers and worlds, and having helped build a few myself, I decided I to bring a new viking land to life. The map was hand picked because of its very large starter island, but most of the challenges lay across the oceans and will require skill and cunning to survive them. If you have only played vanilla before, or even if you have played modded, Valheim Evolved will still feel brand new in many ways. As a lot of Valheim's modded community is driven by player creativity there will be opportunities to grow and evolve the game with your ideas. Come join us and see if you can survive!
