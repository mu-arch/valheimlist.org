You were summoned through a mysterious portal unknown to the seed settlers. Your quest will start in the "Seed Island" where settlers will assist to help you get started and learn the basics of how the world works in the seed. Learn the reasons of why you were called out in this world.

Features

A class based, quests driven progression setup. Maximized KG's Marketplace Revamped features to create an RPG feel in the game. Disabled building by default and unlockable only via zone/territory system. Overhauled all weapons and armors through WackysDatabase mod. Disabled all weapons and armor crafting and options only to upgrade with fixed requirements for each equipment piece. Customized armor sets exclusively for each MO classes. Utilizes EpicMMOSystem and ItemRequiresSkillLevel mods on character equipment progression. Biomes are set to be equal making each bosses similar, abandoning the original concept of biome progression of the game. Weekly PVP/PVE challenge.
