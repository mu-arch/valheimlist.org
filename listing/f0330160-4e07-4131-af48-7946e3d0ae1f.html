About the Server

Welcome to Havi's Respite. Haldor has returned home to his people the "Plains Folk" and they are a little more "persuasive" then usual. A lot of villagers have left for the Plains to trade, and never returned! Help us get to the bottom of this adventurer. Be wary, that is just the beginning of all the crazy things going on in Havi's Respite!
