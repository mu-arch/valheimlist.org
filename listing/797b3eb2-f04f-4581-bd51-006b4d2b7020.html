Greetings, Viking! • To join us on the server, download our build and install it in the game folder. Step-by-step instructions on our website: http://valheim-paradox.ru/ It is recommended to bet on a clean version of the game.

Features

• PARADOX House - installed on Spawn. In it you can meet: - Trading platform (Market - trading between players); - Merchant (You can buy potions, food, etc.); - PARADOX Lottery (Try your luck and buy a lottery. Now there are 6 types available); - Quests (You can accept quests and get a reward); - Portal to the PVP Arena.

About the Server

Changes: 📝Chat. More convenient than standard; ♻️Updating resources, ores, etc.; ⚔️Realistic behavior of teleports and caves. So that mobs can go out and enter the dungeons, follow you into the portal - if very close to the player at that moment. I went into the cave to the troll, got angry, thought to go out and rest, but got a club on the head at the exit🙃; 🌾Convenient cultivator. Allows you to automatically bind all the plants planted by the cultivator and place them in a grid in which they will have enough space to grow; 🕰Date and time. Displaying the in-game day and time in the HUD above the minimap; 🧱New construction elements; 🗂Filter categories in the "Create" menu (two-handed, shield, bow, etc.); 👤View from the 1st person (from the 3rd you can also play everything); 🪜Fix narrow stairs; 🪨Any ore can be loaded into the blast furnace (not to be confused with a smelter); ⛵ ⛵New ships; 🆘The ability to raise a partner after death.
