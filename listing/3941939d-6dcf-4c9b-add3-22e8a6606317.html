CleverHeim is a fresh seed started May 12th, and we’re trying to pick up players who want to have a chill time playing through the game.

Features

In the near future, I will be implementing a system to appoint Jarls. It will basically be a vote among the current Jarls when someone declares his intent to become one. Only Jarls will have the authority to establish wards. Any ward found that does not belong to a Jarl will be destroyed, and the culprit judged by a council of Jarls. Each jarl will control a Jarldom, a region of land (often an island or large chunk of island. Jarls blessed in one resource can trade with Jarls not-so-blessed in that resource, but more blessed in another. Jarls may establish Clans. Each member of the clan is subject entirely to the Jarl’s rules. Jarls establish criteria for both entrance and departure from the Clan, as well as any code of rules. For example, a Jarl might task Clan members with helping out with gardening or resource-gathering, another Jarl might have weekly PvP tournaments, another jarl might forbid PvP altogether.

About the Server

Valheim veterans and newbies alike are welcome. Eventually we plan to increase the server cap, and the earlier you join, the better chance you have to be Jarl of your own village.
