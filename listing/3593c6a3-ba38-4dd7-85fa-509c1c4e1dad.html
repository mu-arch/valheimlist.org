About the Server

In Valheim Reloaded you will have the opportunity to develop your character and get more powerful as you progres. There is 100s of variations on how to setup your gear and what stats your character should get once you level up by completing 100´s of quests, side quests and battlepass. Instead of just a linear upgrading scale of armor and weapons you now have the choice between multiple armors and enhancements. In Reloaded you´ll face different kinds of monsters with different levels and abilities (poison, fire, frost, spirit or chaos damage). You´ll never know what to expect and what will cross your path. Be a part of community events that have been updated with more variations and monsters. Through our discord server, you can easily apply to get whitelisted. Getting started is very easy with good guides on how to get started.
