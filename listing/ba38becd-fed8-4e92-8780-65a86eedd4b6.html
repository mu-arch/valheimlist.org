Valheim Philippines is primarily focused on survival PVP or MMORTS, so you may raid someone's area or form a team to grow strength against other players. The server will not stray too far from the original valheim's game mechanics (will not put any inappropriate mods).

Features

1. Server Side Character (Personal chracter only within the server). 2. Currently have a 100 maximum of in-game players slot (Will probably increased soon if needed). 3. Factions. 4. Raids. 5. Forced PvP. 6. Removed Boss Stones (This has been deleted in order to balance the PVP in-game but bosses are still present in the game, and you can spawn and engage them. Will implement another way to use boss powers) 7. Portals are no longer available (To make traveling more fun and interactive gaming and also avoiding some abuse). 8. Using world of Middle Earth BC.
