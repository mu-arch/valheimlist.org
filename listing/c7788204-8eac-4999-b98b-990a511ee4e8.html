About the Server

Looking to creating a Viking empire. There are 5 Yarls in-game. There will be regions cut out based on the area you claim. You are free to rule them how you see fit. Skirmishes will be allowed based on a declaration of war. If you decide to go at it yourself your wishes will be respected. This server is meant for all players from casual to hardcore.
