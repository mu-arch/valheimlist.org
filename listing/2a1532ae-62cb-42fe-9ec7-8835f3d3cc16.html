Comfy is a community driven & vanilla friendly Valheim server. We have massive PvE and PvP events, and have had the largest population since launch, with as many as 116 players online at once. Comfy has multiple highly involved programmers from Google and elsewhere creating content and tools.

Features

Balanced, high performance server backed, and customized content: - Working & Functional Economy - Automatically replenished resources - Custom Guilds - Raids - Dungeons - Labyrinth - Jumping Puzzles - Staff/Player hosted events

About the Server

In the beginning, Comfy was just a server I set up for a friend. It was quite humble, just a few guys playing through the game together, hosted on my home computer. I decided to put the server on a dedicated box so we could all play at odd hours and something amazing happened. We just started growing day by day with people inviting their friends and sharing us around Facebook and elsewhere. The rest is history. Comfy has grown to be the largest server in the world with a dedicated game master team, development team, and moderation staff. We like to keep things "comfy". That means befriending and helping out new players, and consoling our long-timers through hardships. In the game, and beyond. When you join Comfy, you're joining more than just a gaming community.

About the Staff

Comfy is operated by Saurek, who is also the creator of Valheimlist.org. We have multiple high profile software engineers on our staff, and many of our server's mods are completely custom, written by the team. The game master and moderator team is picked from highly involved community members.
